WANDERLUST VOYAGES — TOUR PACKAGE CATALOG (2026 SEASON)

Company overview:
Wanderlust Voyages is a full-service tour operator offering curated packages across Asia, Africa, and Europe.
All prices are per person in USD, based on double occupancy unless noted. Prices include accommodation, listed
activities, internal transfers, and the services of a local guide where specified. International airfare is NOT
included unless stated. Children under 12 receive a 30% discount when sharing a room with two adults. Single
travelers pay a single-occupancy supplement (typically 35% of the package price). Group bookings of 6+ travelers
receive a 10% discount. Travel insurance is mandatory and can be arranged for an additional fee.

Booking codes follow the pattern COUNTRY-THEME-DURATION (e.g. THA-BEACH-07).

==================================================================================================
GENERAL NOTES FOR TRAVEL AGENTS
==================================================================================================

- Lead time: international packages should be booked at least 6 weeks in advance; peak seasons (Japan cherry
  blossom, Kenya migration, Swiss December) require 3+ months.
- Deposits: 25% non-refundable deposit confirms a booking; balance due 30 days before departure.
- Customization: any package can be extended, shortened, or combined (e.g. THA-MIX-09 + IDN-HONEY-07 as a
  two-country trip). Contact the operations desk for custom quotes.
- Visa & entry: most destinations offer visa-on-arrival or e-visa for common nationalities; always confirm based
  on the customer's passport.
- Best value: shoulder-season departures (e.g. Bali in April or October, Switzerland in June) offer the same
  itineraries at lower demand.
